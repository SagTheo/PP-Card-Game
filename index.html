Rules of the game

×
  • When the button 'Play' is clicked, a new game begins
  • You will have 5 seconds to memorise the numbers on the cards before they disappear
  • You will then have to click on a card to reveal the number
  • If you find a pair, the numbers will stay on the cards
  • If you don't, all the numbers will disappear again
  • If you find all the pairs, you win. That will add 1 to the counter of wins
  • If you make the numbers disappear 3 times, you lose. That will add 1 to the counter of losses
  • If you lose 3 times, it is game over. The counter of wins and losses will be reset to zero
